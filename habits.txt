7 habits:

1. Habit:
Programing is physically demanding activity!
Drink water, exercise, controls your posture, get some sunshine.
Protect your body. 

2. Habit: 
Get it done attitude.
Make sure you don't end up in some neverending loot. Do your work, finish it and send it out!

3. Habit: 
Keep it simple - consistency
Make sure your work/code is simple, easily readable for you and other programmers.

4. Habit:
Consistent way to get into your zone.
The best way for programmers to be efficient is to be able to code in the "zone", so you won't be disturbed every 10 minutes.

5. Habit: 
Always learn new things!
You need to keep yourself up to date, with the newest trends.
